Current projects

Master thesis

After starting the last step of my master program in february 2025, I am doing the research for my master thesis for a total of 9 months. My deadline is now approaching, as I'm writing this; deadline: january 2026. For the reearch I moved to Stockholm, Sweden, where I am now doing my thesis at the Oskar Klein Centre. My thesis is in the field of cosmology; more specifically, I am trying to improve the parameter constraints from cosmological parameter estimation by including Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) observations. In doing do, we hope to improve paramter constraints and are especially interested in the resulting contraint on the sum of neutrino masses.

Micro-songs

This is a long living idea of mine, where I record original pieces of music. Starting with guitar and possibly adding a bass and drum- track if I ca acces this easily through basic software. The idea is to become better at 'writing' music by making short compositions, made up from 3 to 5 guitar tracks and a basic rythm section. By making small pieces in different genres this will not only push my creativity on the guitar but also help me to just flop out some chords, see what keys and melodies work with it and expose myself to different styles of guitar playing. In doing so, I can also see whether the the process of recording is fun and if I want to push myself to create more extensive pieces.
