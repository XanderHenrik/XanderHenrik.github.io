This features a little project idea of mine that lives in the back of the mind for a while now. This originated on the steel, two-wheeled horse that I used to ride around in Utrecht.

Introducing: Utrecht by bike, a foto series from the cyclists perspective.
This is a foto series that is all about a spontaneous frame, spotted from the bike. Usually when you spot the frame, you immediately want to take a picture of it, however, since we are not allowed to use our phones on the bike, it is stowed away. This makes that the series of pictures, making up "Utrecht by bike", contains a lot of sub-optimal frames, motion blur and a perspective from the bikelane. Occasionally, the frame can be worth stopping for, or there is one up for grabs at a traffic light.
With this, a small call-out to enjoy the surroundings more as you move trhough. The pictures I took might not be convincing, but the world should be, give it a try!

Naturally, upon moving to Stockholm, I continued this in a new environment, a new perspective and maybe some things that seem more interesting to me than they should, but that is what you get for being a tourist hehe.



Utrecht by bike

Utrecht is a beautiful city to bike through. Around many corners you can find very nice looking and characteristic buildings and streets. No unnessecarily high buildings and lively at all times.




Stockholm by bike

Although maybe not expected to be nice for biking, Stockholm does provide infrsastructure and such nice colors in the buildings and parks. This makes the city even nice looking on a gray, rainiy day. Nest to the colors, the water adds so much to the city.
